CV Template

If you want your CV to stand out a bit more with certain formatting options, we've provided a cheatsheet of sorts to help with that.
These aren't 100% necessary but it could help your CV information look better.
play around a little and have a look at the preview. Have fun!

header: ## your text
bold: **your text**
italics: *your text*
bullet point list:
-your text
-text2
-text3
numbered list:
1. your text
2. text2
3. text3
links: [link name](https://linkto-site.com)
you can also add a title for this link when hovering over it, this would look like this:
[link name](https://linkto-site.com "this is the link to my site")
